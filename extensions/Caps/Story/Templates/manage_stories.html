Have you wanted a safe space to talk to colleagues about medical abortion, your practice, the difficulties or what has worked for you?  Our Peer-to-peer discussion room is here!

Please note:
  • Experiences should not include information that will identify specific patients.
  • For research purposes we may use your experience post, without any of your identifying information, to advocate with health system and policy leaders to improve policies and systems that support your provision of safe, accessible medical abortion care.
  • You are welcome to start a new experience, or to comment on experiences others have posted.
  • You may add your name and/or contact info to your case or comments, or may post anonymously.
  • If you include any patient identifiers or inappropriate information, an administrator will contact you asking you to revise your case.
  • Your case will not appear to other members until it has a site administrator has approved it.
