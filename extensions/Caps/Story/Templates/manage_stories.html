This page is a place where you can share your experience with a difficult or frustrating case, or perhaps where the logistics to manage medical abortion care were challenging. Experiences should not include information that will identify specific patients. You can choose to add your name or contact information, or to post anonymously. For research purposes we may use your experience post, without any of your identifying information, to advocate with health system and policy leaders to improve policies and systems that support your provision of safe, accessible medical abortion care. You are welcome to start a new experience, or to comment on experiences others have posted.

Members who post a case or story may choose to add their name and/or contact info to comments, but may post anonymously.

If you have a case to share click "Share a Case" and complete the fields. The cases will not show up in the member cases until they have been approved.
